Purpose of the Program

Kindergarten Space Program is to help ignite and satisfy curisosity about space and what lies beyond through documentary videos and the basic information on the planets within our solar system, starting with the sun, moon, and earth itself while making it fun and interesting throughout our program. Our goal here is to make space fun and entertaing for curious young minds.

  • Learn about what the solar system is
  • Learn about how the Sun keeps Earth flourishing
  • Learn how the Moon helps the Earth
  • Learn about the planet we grow up on Earth
